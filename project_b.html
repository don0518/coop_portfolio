Role

UI design, UX design, functionality design, interaction design, visual design, branding + identity

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Visual Studio Code (HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT), GitKraken

Stage 1 - Researching & Creating Style Guide

Before making the webpage's style guide, my role is to research the existing beer craft company and their target audiences. The research helped us to develop the theme of the website and what features we need for it. We integrated the findings of different beer craft company and came out with our brand: Container Brewing. (Above picture shows the style guide's development of the website)

Stage 2 - Content & Interface Design

Once we finished the style guide, we begin to structure what we need for the webpage. As we came out with four different pages for the webpage, we begin to sketch different layout and implement them to the actual mockup by applying HTML and CSS. My role was to design the home, event, and product detail page. I follow visual looks from the style guide to make the interface consistent and I applied the flex-box property for the later responsive process.

Stage 3 - Applying Responsive Page

After we completed the structure and appearance of the webpage, my role at this stage is to complete the responsiveness of all pages. I applied media queries and edit flexbox properties for different structures in different device sizes. The reason is that we want to focus on the accessibility for all potential users, so they can access from different sizes of devices. We also focused on user experiences such as interaction cues and affordances.

Self Reflection

In the end, the result went good that we received positive feedback from the instructor and one of the highest grade in the class. During the project, I experienced many technique issues and accessibility issues. However, the project pushed me to develop my problem-solving skills which allow me to solve all these kinds of issues by doing researches.
