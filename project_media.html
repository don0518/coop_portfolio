Media Design Projects

Different experiences of creating media-related designs

1. The Short Documentary Film

The short documentary film, Kitchen on the Ridge follows a small restaurant owner that immigrated to Vancouver, Canada in 1996, in hopes of achieving the American dream. The overarching theme of the documentary is the adaptation from Chinese to Western culture and how having a language barrier can become a huge obstacle to overcome. During the filming, my tasks included Cameraman, Lighting, Editing, and Post-processing. The project allows me to collaborate with the owner of the restaurant and I gained lots of professional experience in making effective communication with clients. On the other hand, I was able to improve my time management skills which manage my schedule wisely due to short-period filming and editing time. (team size: 3)

Project Type

Academic project - Documentary film

Role

Cameraman, Lighting, Editing, and Post-processing

Tools

Adobe PR & AE, DSLR Camera, Small Lighting Kit

2. Visual Effect Video

"Vision" is an individual visual effect project that allows me to practice the workflow of Adobe AE and PR. I spent one month on building this project and its objective aims to present a portfolio-quality work that represents my design philosophy. I combined visual effects and typeface with raw footages that I took in the Great Vancouver and I experienced a lot from managing my schedule with other school projects. Throughout the learning, I benefit a lot from researching adobe tutorials to fulfill my need for technical skills. The experience was a valuable practice to learn the importance of early-stage researching and workflow planning.

Project Type

Academic project - Visual Effect Film

Role

Cameraman, Researching, Planning, Editing, and Post-processing

Tools

Adobe PR & AE, DSLR Camera, GoPro

3. 3D Animation

"Boba" is a 3D animation created through Maya and Adobe PR. The project took one month to complete and I worked as a team leader with other three team members. Since 3D animation takes time on building up models, rigging character's action, and rendering animation, I managed each team member's schedule and assigned different tasks based on their abilities. The project is very time-consuming for a month and I handled it cautiously to avoid mistakes. Although the team followed a well-planned schedule, we still faced challenges from the technical side. We solved issues by researching online resources or find alternative solutions to fulfill the requirement. The project helped my skill to gain valuable assets of working in a team and I learned the importance of communication when I led the team. (team size: 4)

Project Type

Academic project - 3D Animation

Role

Team Leader, Modelling, Rigging, Lighting, and Animated sequence

Tools

Adobe PR, Autodesk Maya
